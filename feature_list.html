Feature List

  • Detailed Dwarf Information Can show attributes, progress towards attributes, as well as progress bars per skill of each dwarf in your fort. Never wonder how many more turns a pump operator needs for level 8.
  • Labor Management Manage labors and professions much more easily than in-game using a flexible UI, allowing quick review of all dwarves at-a-glance
  • Persistent custom professions import and manage any number of custom professions across all your forts. Assign multiple dwarves to a custom profession at once to unify active labors
  • Pending Changes Display all pending changes before they're written to the game giving you a chance to review what is about to be written to game in detail
  • Sorting Sort labor columns by associated skill level, you can also sort dwarfs by happiness etc...
  • Persistent and customizable display change colors, reposition/hide information screens, even create your own views
  • Grouping Group your dwarfs by profession, sex, legendary status, happiness level, or just a big flat list
